# SMU Student Success Risk and Vulnerability Dashboard: 2025 FTEN Cohort

## Overview

This dashboard provides an executive-level analysis of student success risk factors among the 2025 First-Time Entering Students (FTEN) cohort at Sefako Makgatho Health Sciences University (SMU).

The dashboard focuses on strategic student success indicators that are strongly associated with student retention, progression, and academic success.

## Strategic Indicators

The dashboard analyses:

* First-Generation University Students
* Rural versus Urban Background
* Financial Vulnerability and NSFAS Dependency
* Language Diversity and Schooling Background
* Family Responsibilities
* Commuting Challenges

## Outputs

For each indicator the dashboard provides:

* Frequency Tables
* Percentage Tables
* Executive Visualisations
* Downloadable CSV Reports
* Strategic Insights
